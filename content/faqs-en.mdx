## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is the pool heated?**  
No, but it stays warm year-round thanks to Panama's tropical climate.

**Can I check out late?**  
Possibly! Send a message and we’ll do our best to accommodate.

**Where do I throw out trash?**  
There’s a trash chute in the hallway near the elevator.

**Is the tap water safe to drink?**  
Yes, but we provide filtered water for convenience.
