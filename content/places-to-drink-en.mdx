## Bars & Nightlife

The beach communities around Punta Caelo offer a relaxed but fun nightlife scene. From sunset cocktails on the sand to lively sports bars, here's where to grab a drink.

---

### Punta Caelo Beach Club ⭐

**Right at Punta Caelo** • Beach Bar

Our community's own beach club! Walk down from the apartment, grab a cocktail, and watch the sunset over the Pacific. The perfect end to a beach day without going anywhere.

---

### Caelo Beach Restaurant Bar

**Right at Punta Caelo** • Cocktail Bar

The bar at Caelo Beach Restaurant serves craft cocktails using local ingredients. Even if you're not dining, you can grab drinks at the bar with stunning ocean views.

**Hours:** Wed-Sun (see restaurant hours) • [WhatsApp +507 6105-9826](https://wa.me/50761059826)

---

### La Ruina

**Gorgona** • Beach Bar • Live Music

A legendary beach bar in nearby Gorgona known for authentic Panamanian food, cold beers, and lively atmosphere. Great spot for sunset drinks and meeting locals and expats alike.

---

### Picasso Bar

**Coronado** • Expat Bar

The go-to hangout for the expat community. Features happy hour on Wednesdays, trivia night and open mic on Thursdays. Multiple screens for watching sports and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

---

### Loco's Bar & Grill

**Coronado** • Sports Bar

Popular Friday night destination with large screens for sporting events and special events throughout the week. Good pub food and cold drinks.

---

### The Tiki Bar

**Gorgona Beach** • Beach Bar

Casual beach bar right on the sand in Gorgona. Open Friday evenings (4-10pm) and weekends (Sat-Sun 12-6pm). Perfect for a relaxed afternoon drink with your feet in the sand.

---

### Bahia Beach Bar

**Gorgona** • Beach Bar

Laid-back beach bar serving excellent ceviche and mojitos. A great spot for a casual afternoon overlooking the waves.

---

### Hotel Decameron Coronado

**Coronado** • Resort Bar

The Coronado location of the Decameron chain. Day passes may be available - call ahead to confirm availability and pricing.

---

## Buenaventura

**Buenaventura** • Resort Bars

## Farallon

### Mia Beach Bar

**Farallon** • Beach Bar/Brew Pub

Right on the beach at Mia Beach restaurant. Great selection of domestic, imported, and artisanal draft beers plus cocktails. Perfect sunset spot with occasional live music and events.

**Hours:** Daily 9am-9pm • [WhatsApp +507 6174-6241](https://wa.me/50761746241)

## Playa Blanca

### Hotel RIU Playa Blanca

**Playa Blanca** • Resort Bars

Large all-inclusive resort with multiple bars including a swim-up pool bar, beach bar, and nightclub. Day passes available for non-guests - unlimited drinks included with your pass.

**Features:** Swim-up bar, poolside bars, nightclub, beach bar

**Day pass:** Call ahead for current pricing and availability

**Contact:** +507 907-0900

---

### Grand Decameron Panama

**Playa Blanca** • Resort Bars

The flagship Decameron resort in Panama with 7 pools and multiple bars. Day passes include unlimited alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

**Day pass:** ~$60-65/adult, ~$38 kids 5-11, free under 5 • 10am-5pm

**Includes:** All bars, 7 pools, beach access, buffet lunch

**Book ahead:** Limited capacity, especially weekends/holidays
