## Garbage Bins

There is a garbage bin outside by the elevator for the community.

## Cooling

We have split air conditioning in each room. Please only turn on the air conditioner when you are in the room.

## Parking

There is one assigned parking spot for each unit.

## Cleaning Supplies

We have cleaning supplies in the kitchen cabinets at ground level, there are mops, brooms, and cleaning supplies in the maids quarters.

## Pool

The pool is open from 8am to 8pm.
