## Beaches

There is a beautiful beach directly in front of the community. Go to the 4th floor, turn right towards the pool area, and follow the stairs down to the beach club.

### Playa Coronado

This tranquil beach offers striking black and white sand, ideal for swimming and scenic walks. Access points are near [Blue Bay Coronado Golf and Beach Resort](https://www.bluebaycoronado.com/), with nearby street parking. Perfect spot for sunrise and sunset photography.

### Playa Serena, Gorgona

A beautiful bay with gentle waves, great for swimming and beach volleyball. Enjoy the weekend restaurant and snack bar at Bahia Condominiums or the local fare at a nearby Fonda.

### Punta Chame

Famous for excellent surfing and kite surfing, featuring long stretches of white sand. Consider breakfast at Casa Amarillo before visiting surf schools and [Nitro City Action Sports Resort](https://www.nitrocitypanama.com/).

### Playa El Palmar

Ideal for surfing and swimming, offering beginner surf lessons. A small weekend entry fee provides access to showers, bathrooms, and a charming restaurant with ocean views.

### Playa Santa Clara

Stunning white sand beach popular with tourists and expats, offering fresh seafood restaurants and bohio rentals with hammocks. Small parking and entry fee required.

### Playa Grande

A tranquil hidden gem across from the El Valle entrance road. Stay overnight and relax at [Istmo Adventure Retreat](https://www.istmoretreat.com/).

### Playa Farallón

Wide, beautiful white sand beaches with activities including horseback riding and jet skiing. Popular [Hotel Royal Decameron Golf Beach Resort Villas](https://www.decameron.com/en/otr-destinations/panama/farallon/royal-decameron-panama) offers numerous amenities. One of the best entry points is near Nico’s Beach, which has a beachfront bar and restaurant perfect for casual dining and sunset views.

### Playa Ensenada, San Carlos

Secluded beach with a picturesque white sand shoreline and affordable day passes for pool and beach access at TurismoCentro. Fresh fish available from local fishermen near the entrance.

## Restaurants

Enjoy various local dining options offering delicious Panamanian and international cuisines, perfect for both casual dining and special occasions.

## Shopping

Explore shopping opportunities nearby, including local markets for souvenirs, groceries, and other essentials, as well as boutiques and specialty stores.

## Activities

Participate in various activities like golfing, horseback riding, hiking, fishing, and more, providing entertainment and adventure for all ages.

## Tours

Discover guided tours available for exploring natural attractions, local culture, historical landmarks, and wildlife encounters, enhancing your Panama experience.
