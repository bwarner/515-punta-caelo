## Friendly Reminders

### Respect for the Community

- **Quiet Hours:** 11:00 PM - 8:00 AM
- No parties or events without prior authorization.
- Keep music at a moderate volume at all times.
- No unregistered guests allowed.

### Air Conditioning

- Keep doors and windows closed when the AC is on.
- Turn off the air conditioning when leaving the apartment.

### Property Care

- Please treat the apartment as your own. Report any damage or issues immediately.
- Do not enter the apartment with sand. Please rinse off at the outdoor shower or clean your feet before entering.
- Do not sit on sofas, beds, or chairs while wet.
- No smoking inside the apartment.
- Please don't rearrange the furniture.
- Please do your dishes.
- Please take the trash out before you leave.

### Towel Policy

- The apartment provides both beach/pool towels and apartment towels.
- Do not use apartment towels for the beach, pool, makeup removal, grease, shoes, floors, or any use that may damage them.
- Please put towels back in their place before checkout.
- Do not hang towels on the balcony where they are externally visible.

### Pets

- No pets allowed without prior authorization.

### Keys

- Please take extra care of your keys. Lost keys incur a replacement fee of $50.

### Check-in & Check-out

- Check-in: 3:00 PM
- Check-out: 11:00 AM
- Late check-out only with prior approval.
