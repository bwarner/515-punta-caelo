We kindly ask you to check-out before 11:00 AM. If you'd like to request a late check-out, please contact us. However, please be informed that we may not be able to grant your request if we have a same-day check-in.

## Checkout Checklist

- Turn off all lights, heat/AC
- Load dishes in the dishwasher
- Close all windows and doors
- Place all trash in the bin outside
- Leave keys on the kitchen counter
- Lock the door by pressing the Yale emblem on the keypad
