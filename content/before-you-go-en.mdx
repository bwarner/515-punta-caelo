## Departure Checklist

We hope you enjoyed your stay! Before you leave, please help us prepare the space for the next guests:

- 🧺 Place used towels in the bathroom hamper
- 🛏 Strip bed linens and leave them on the beds
- 🗑 Take out the trash (chute near the elevator)
- 🔑 Return keys to the front gate or building staff

Please send us a quick WhatsApp when you’ve checked out. Your feedback means a lot!

> Check-out is at **11:00 AM sharp** unless otherwise arranged.
