## Restaurants

The San Carlos and Coronado area offers a diverse dining scene, from world-class beachfront restaurants to authentic local fondas. Here are our top recommendations within a short drive.

---

### Caelo Beach Restaurant ⭐

**Right at Punta Caelo** • Fine Dining • $$$

Upscale beachfront restaurant right in the Punta Caelo community. Head chefs José Carles (formerly of Donde José) and Erasmo Bosquez (formerly of Pujol, Mexico City) create an ever-evolving menu of locally-inspired gourmet cuisine.

**Must-try dishes:** Lobster Roll with pickled Mamon Chino, spicy fried chicken sandwich, green plantain churros

**Hours:** Wed-Thu 6-10pm, Fri 12-10pm, Sat-Sun 9-11:30am & 12-10pm (closed Mon-Tue)

---

### Picasso Restaurant

**Coronado** • International • $$-$$$

A beloved expat hangout with soul. Picasso offers international cuisine, ocean views, and a lively atmosphere. Known for their Wednesday happy hour, Thursday trivia nights, and excellent pizza.

---

### Trattoria Fiorentina

**Coronado** • Italian • $$

Winner of the "Best Pizza at the Beaches" competition. Authentic Italian cuisine with handmade pasta and wood-fired pizzas in a cozy setting.

---

### El Rincón del Chef

**San Carlos** • Panamanian/Seafood • $$

Fresh local seafood and traditional Panamanian dishes. A favorite among locals for corvina (sea bass) and langostinos.

---

### Delicias Margot

**San Carlos** • Panamanian • $

Authentic Panamanian home cooking in a classic fonda style. Great for an affordable, filling meal of rice, beans, and fresh-cooked meats.

---

### El Parque Restaurante

**San Carlos** • Chinese-Panamanian • $

Located in front of San Carlos park. A local institution serving generous portions of Chinese-Panamanian fusion at budget-friendly prices.

---

### Terrazas del Mar

**Vista Mar Resort** • Fine Dining • $$$

Elegant beachfront dining at Vista Mar Golf & Beach Resort. Perfect for a special occasion with stunning ocean views.

---

### Cholos

**Coronado** • Mexican • $$

Authentic Mexican food at the beach. Great margaritas and generous portions of tacos, burritos, and enchiladas.

---

### Nacionsushi Coronado

**Coronado** • Japanese/Asian Fusion • $$

Located in Panama's first beach community. Over 150 menu options including keto and gluten-friendly dishes, combining Japanese and Southeast Asian cuisine in a modern setting.

---

## Gorgona

### La Ruina Restaurant

**Gorgona** • Panamanian • $$

More than just a bar - La Ruina serves authentic Panamanian food at reasonable prices in a beachfront setting. Great for a casual lunch or dinner.

---

### Bahia Restaurant

**Gorgona** • Seafood • $$

Fresh ceviche and seafood dishes with ocean views. A laid-back spot popular with locals and visitors alike.

## Buenaventura

_Note: Buenaventura is a gated resort community. Restaurant reservations are recommended and may be required for entry._

### El Faro Beach Club

**Buenaventura Resort** • Seafood • $$$

Beachfront dining at The Buenaventura Golf & Beach Resort. Fresh seafood with stunning ocean views. Reservations recommended.

---

### Buenaventura Puntarena Beach Club

**Buenaventura** • Casual Dining • $$

Steps from the sea in the heart of Puntarena. Great food, refreshing drinks, and pool access. Open Fri-Sun 9am-5pm and during holidays. Call ahead for entry.

## Farallon

### Mia Beach

**Farallon** • Italian/Pizza • $$

Authentic Italian pizza made with imported ingredients by an Italian couple, right on the beach. The first beachfront commercial plaza in Panama featuring artisan beer, gelato, and stunning ocean views.

**Must-try:** Their signature Mia Pizza, craft beers, and Italian desserts

**Hours:** Daily 9am-9pm

## Playa Blanca

### Playa Blanca Beach Resort

**Playa Blanca** • All-Inclusive Resort • $$$

Beachfront resort with the largest saltwater pool in Central America, 4 restaurants, and a marina. Day passes available but note: the main pool is for overnight guests only.

**Day pass:** ~$75/person (call to confirm current pricing)

**Includes:** Beach access, restaurants, snack bar

**Note:** Beach towels not included ($5 rental)

**Website:** [playablancaresort.com](https://www.playablancaresort.com) • <TrackedWhatsappLink venue="playa-blanca-resort" phone="50769345141" locale="en" source="places-to-eat">WhatsApp +507 6934-5141</TrackedWhatsappLink>

---

### Hotel RIU Playa Blanca

**Playa Blanca** • All-Inclusive Resort • $$$

Large all-inclusive resort with 4 restaurants serving international, Asian, Italian, and steakhouse cuisine. Day passes available for non-guests - a great option for a full resort experience with beach, pools, and unlimited food & drinks.

**Features:** 3 pools, waterslide, swim-up bar, beachfront dining

**Day pass:** Call ahead for current pricing and availability

**Contact:** +507 907-0900

---

### Grand Decameron Panama

**Playa Blanca** • All-Inclusive Resort • $$$

The flagship Decameron resort in Panama with 8 restaurants and buffet dining. Day passes include unlimited food and drinks - one of the most popular day pass destinations in Panama.

**Day pass:** ~$60-65/adult, ~$38 kids 5-11, free under 5 • 10am-5pm

**Includes:** 8 restaurants, buffet lunch, snacks, 7 pools, beach access

**Book ahead:** Limited capacity, especially weekends/holidays
