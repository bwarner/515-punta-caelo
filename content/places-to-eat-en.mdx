## Recommended Restaurants

### 🍽 Casual & Local
- **La Ruina** – Pizza, burgers, great for families
- **Nazca 21** – Peruvian cuisine and fresh ceviche
- **Café Coronado** – Breakfasts, pastries, coffee

### 🍷 Upscale Options
- **Luna Rossa** – Italian, pasta, wine list
- **La Casa de Lourdes** (El Valle) – Destination dining in a colonial villa
- **Restaurante Pescao** – Seafood and cocktails with ocean views
